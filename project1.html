Project 1: InvestiGator (WIP)

The InvestiGator is an investigative tool, made to assist law enforcement or private investigations by storing information in a easy-to-access and organized way. The structure of the organization is made by separating case files, profiles for each person of interest, and profiles of investigators.

This project was born out of my love and interest for investigations and in combining web technologies with real-world data.

  • Technologies: JavaScript, Bootstrap, REST APIs
  • Roles: Full-stack Developer, UI/UX Designer
